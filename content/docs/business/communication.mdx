## Communication Timeline

### Pre-Project

**Application Phase:**
- Respond within 24-48 hours
- Answer questions promptly
- Set clear expectations

**Selection Phase:**
- Inform of decision quickly
- Provide next steps
- Schedule kickoff

### During Project

**Weekly Check-ins:**
- Schedule regular touchpoints
- Review progress
- Address blockers
- Provide feedback

**Ad-hoc Communication:**
- Available for questions
- Respond within 24 hours
- Provide guidance when needed

**Milestone Reviews:**
- Scheduled review meetings
- Detailed feedback
- Approve or request revisions

### Post-Project

**Completion:**
- Final review and approval
- Provide feedback and endorsement
- Discuss next steps (if any)

**Follow-up:**
- Request testimonial
- Offer letter of recommendation
- Stay connected

## Best Practices

### Be Clear and Specific

**Instead of:**
"The design needs work."

**Try:**
"The color scheme looks great! For the homepage layout, could you:
1. Increase the hero image size by 20%
2. Move the call-to-action button above the fold
3. Reduce the padding around the testimonials section"

## Common Scenarios

### Student Has Questions

**Good Response:**
"Great question! Here's how to approach this...
[Detailed explanation]
Let me know if you need clarification!"

### Student Misses Deadline

**Good Response:**
"I noticed the milestone wasn't submitted by Friday. Is everything 
okay? Let's discuss any challenges you're facing and adjust the 
timeline if needed."

### Work Needs Revision

**Good Response:**
"Thanks for submitting! This is a solid start. Here's specific 
feedback for the next iteration:
[Detailed, actionable feedback]
When can you have the revisions ready?"

### Student Exceeds Expectations

**Good Response:**
"This is excellent work! You really went above and beyond with 
[specific example]. Your attention to detail and creativity really 
shine through. Thank you!"

## Handling Difficult Situations

### Student Not Responding

1. Send friendly follow-up
2. Wait 48 hours
3. Send second message with concern
4. If still no response after 5 days, consider backup plan

### Quality Issues

1. Provide specific, constructive feedback
2. Offer resources or guidance
3. Set clear expectations for improvement
4. Consider if project scope was appropriate

### Timeline Conflicts

1. Discuss openly and early
2. Adjust milestones if reasonable
3. Consider reducing scope
4. Document any agreed changes

## Pro Tips

- Encourage questions
