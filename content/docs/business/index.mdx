## Common Questions

### How long does it take to find a student?

Most projects receive applications within 3-7 days. Well-defined projects with clear requirements typically receive 5-15 quality applications.

### What makes a good project for students?

Good student projects are:
- Well-scoped (completable in stated timeframe)
- Clearly defined with specific deliverables
- Appropriate for student skill levels
- Provide learning value
- Have real-world impact

### Should I offer compensation?

While not required, offering a stipend (even small) can:
- Attract more applications
- Signal seriousness and professionalism
- Show appreciation for quality work
- Increase commitment and quality

### How do I ensure project success?

Success factors include:
- Clear project definition
- Regular communication
- Timely feedback
- Available mentorship
- Realistic expectations
- Professional conduct
