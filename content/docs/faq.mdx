# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SkillBridge.

## General Questions

### What is SkillBridge?

SkillBridge is a platform that connects students with real-world project opportunities from organizations. It helps students gain practical experience while helping organizations find talented individuals for their projects.

### Is SkillBridge free to use?

Yes! SkillBridge is completely free for both students and organizations.

### Who can use SkillBridge?

SkillBridge is designed for:
- Students looking for project opportunities
- Educational institutions
- Organizations seeking student talent

## Account & Profile

### How do I create an account?

1. **Visit the homepage** - Go to the SkillBridge website
2. **Click Sign Up** - Choose your account type (Student/Organization)
3. **Complete registration** - Fill in your information and verify your email

### Can I edit my profile after creating it?

Yes! You can update your profile information at any time by going to your profile settings.

### What should I include in my profile?

For the best results, include:
- A professional profile picture
- Detailed educational background
- Relevant work experience
- Skills and expertise
- Portfolio or project links

## Projects & Applications

### How do I find projects?

Use the **Browse** page to search and filter projects by:
- Keywords
- Categories
- Skills required
- Project status

### How many projects can I apply to?

There's no limit! You can apply to as many projects as you're interested in and qualified for.

### Can I withdraw an application?

Currently, you cannot withdraw an application once submitted. Please make sure you're committed before applying.

### How will I know if my application is accepted?

You'll receive notifications when there's an update on your application status. You can also check the status in your profile dashboard.

## Technical Issues

### The website isn't loading properly. What should I do?

Try these steps:
1. Clear your browser cache and cookies
2. Try a different browser
3. Check your internet connection
4. Disable browser extensions temporarily

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password via email.

### I'm not receiving verification emails

Check your spam/junk folder. If you still don't see it, contact support for assistance.

## Contact & Support

### How do I contact support?

For any issues or questions not covered in this FAQ, please reach out to the SkillBridge team through the contact information provided on the website.

### How can I report a bug or suggest a feature?

We welcome feedback! Please submit bug reports and feature suggestions through our GitHub repository or contact our support team.
