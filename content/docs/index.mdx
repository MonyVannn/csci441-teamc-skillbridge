## Overview

SkillBridge bridges the gap between academic learning and professional experience by providing a marketplace where students can find meaningful projects and organizations can access skilled talent. This documentation will guide you through every aspect of the platform, from initial setup to advanced features.

## Platform Capabilities

### Core Features

**Project Marketplace**

Browse and discover projects filtered by category, required skills, and timeline. The marketplace features advanced search capabilities, real-time updates, and personalized recommendations based on your profile.

**User Profiles**

Create comprehensive profiles showcasing skills, experience, education, and completed projects. Profiles support portfolio integration, skill verification, and professional endorsements.

**Communication System**

Built-in messaging facilitates direct communication between students and organizations. Features include real-time chat, conversation history, and integrated notifications.

**Progress Tracking**

Monitor your journey on the platform with detailed analytics, achievement badges, and a verifiable portfolio of completed work.
