## Quick Start: Students

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit the [SkillBridge homepage](/)
2. Click **Sign Up** in the top right corner
3. Choose **Student Account**
4. Complete registration with:
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Or use social login (Google, GitHub, etc.)
5. Verify your email address

> **ðŸ’¡ Tip**: Use a professional email address that you check regularly!

### Step 2: Build Your Profile

A complete profile increases your chances of being selected:

#### Essential Information
- **Profile Photo**: Professional headshot or clear photo
- **Bio**: Brief introduction (2-3 sentences)
- **Contact Information**: How organizations can reach you

#### Education Details
- School/University name
- Degree or major
- Expected or actual graduation date
- Relevant coursework (optional but recommended)

#### Skills & Experience
- List your technical skills
- Include soft skills (communication, teamwork, etc.)
- Add any relevant work experience or internships
- Link to your portfolio, GitHub, or LinkedIn

> **âš ï¸ Important**: Complete at least 70% of your profile before applying to projects!

### Step 3: Explore Projects

Navigate to the **Browse** page to start exploring:

1. **Use Search** - Enter keywords related to your interests
2. **Apply Filters** - Select categories and skills
3. **Read Descriptions** - Understand project requirements
4. **Check Timelines** - Ensure you can commit to the duration
5. **Review Requirements** - Verify you have the necessary skills

### Step 4: Apply to Your First Project

When you find a project that matches your skills:

1. Click on the project card to view details
2. Read the full description and requirements
3. Click **Apply**
4. Fill out the application form with:
   - Why you're interested
   - Relevant experience
   - How you'll approach the project
   - Your availability
5. Submit your application
6. Track status in your dashboard

> **âœ¨ Pro Tip**: Customize each application to the specific project. Generic applications are less likely to be accepted!

### Step 5: Start Your Journey

Once accepted to a project:

1. **Communicate**: Use the integrated chat to discuss details
2. **Clarify Expectations**: Ensure you understand deliverables
3. **Set Milestones**: Break the project into manageable tasks
4. **Update Progress**: Keep the organization informed
5. **Complete & Get Verified**: Finish strong and earn your badge!

## Quick Start: Businesses & Organizations

### Step 1: Create Organization Account

1. Visit the [SkillBridge homepage](/)
2. Click **Sign Up** in the top right corner
3. Choose **Business Account**
4. Provide:
   - Organization name
   - Your email address
   - Password
   - Or use social login
5. Verify your email address

### Step 2: Complete Organization Profile

Make your organization attractive to students:

#### Basic Information
- **Organization Name**: Official business name
- **Industry**: Select your primary industry
- **Size**: Number of employees
- **Location**: City, state/region, country

#### Detailed Profile
- **About Us**: Organization mission and values (3-4 sentences)
- **Website**: Official website URL
- **Logo**: Professional company logo
- **Contact Information**: Primary contact for students

> **ðŸ’¡ Tip**: Students are more likely to apply to organizations with complete profiles!

### Step 3: Post Your First Project

Create a compelling project posting:

#### Project Basics
- **Project Title**: Clear, descriptive name
- **Category**: Select appropriate category
- **Timeline**: Start date and expected duration
- **Commitment Level**: Hours per week expected

#### Project Details
- **Description**: Detailed explanation of the project (3-4 paragraphs)
- **Deliverables**: Specific outcomes you expect
- **Required Skills**: Technical and soft skills needed
- **Preferred Qualifications**: Nice-to-have skills or experience
- **Compensation**: Stipend amount (if applicable)

#### Clear Expectations
- Project scope and boundaries
- Communication preferences
- Submission requirements
- Evaluation criteria

> **âœ¨ Pro Tip**: Well-defined projects attract better candidates!

### Step 4: Review Applications

When students apply to your project:

1. **View Applications**: Access from your dashboard
2. **Review Profiles**: Check student qualifications
3. **Compare Candidates**: Evaluate multiple applicants
4. **Reach Out**: Use chat to ask questions
5. **Make Selection**: Accept or decline applications

### Step 5: Collaborate & Complete

Working with students:

1. **Onboard**: Provide necessary resources and access
2. **Set Milestones**: Define checkpoints for progress
3. **Maintain Communication**: Regular check-ins
4. **Provide Feedback**: Constructive guidance throughout
5. **Verify Completion**: Endorse student's work when done

## Essential Features to Know

### Dashboard
Your central hub for all SkillBridge activities:
- **Students**: View applications, active projects, and messages
- **Businesses**: Manage projects, review applications, and track progress

### Notifications
Stay updated with:
- Application status changes
- New messages
- Project updates
- Important announcements

### Chat System
Real-time communication:
- Click the chat icon (bottom right)
- Start conversations with students/organizations
- View message history
- Get notifications for new messages
