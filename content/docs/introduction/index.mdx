# What is SkillBridge?

SkillBridge is a comprehensive platform designed to bridge the gap between **students and new graduates** seeking practical experience and **businesses and nonprofits** needing specific, short-term projects completed.

## The Problem We Solve

New graduates and students today face significant challenges:

- **Limited Practical Experience**: The transition from academic learning to real-world application remains difficult
- **Saturated Job Market**: Entry-level positions are increasingly competitive
- **Internship Scarcity**: Traditional internship opportunities are limited and highly competitive
- **Portfolio Gap**: Students struggle to build verifiable portfolios that demonstrate their skills

## Our Mission

**Empower emerging talent** by providing opportunities to build verifiable portfolios and enhance employability in a competitive job market while **connecting organizations** with skilled individuals who can complete specific, well-defined projects.

## How SkillBridge Works

### For Students
1. **Create Your Profile** - Showcase your education, skills, and experience
2. **Browse Projects** - Discover micro-projects that match your interests and skillset
3. **Apply & Complete** - Submit applications and work on real-world projects
4. **Build Your Portfolio** - Gain verified, tangible experience for your resume

### For Businesses & Nonprofits
1. **Post Projects** - Define well-scoped, small-scale projects requiring specific skills
2. **Review Applications** - Access a pool of motivated, skilled students
3. **Collaborate** - Work directly with students through integrated communication tools
4. **Complete Projects** - Get quality work done while helping students gain experience

## Platform Philosophy

SkillBridge operates on the principle that **practical experience is invaluable** for career development. We believe that:

- Real-world projects teach skills that classrooms cannot
- Micro-projects provide manageable entry points for students
- Verified completion builds credibility
- Mutual benefit drives sustainable engagement
