## Creating an Account

Get started in minutes with our streamlined account creation process.

<Cards>
  <Card title="Visit Homepage">
    Navigate to the SkillBridge homepage and locate the Sign Up button in the top navigation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Choose Account Type">
    Select Student Account or Business Account based on your intended use of the platform.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Provide Information">
    Enter your email address, create a secure password, or use social login for convenience.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Verify Email">
    Check your inbox for the verification email and click the confirmation link to activate your account.
  </Card>
</Cards>

## Setting Up Your Profile

Complete your profile to maximize your success on the platform.

<Tabs items={['Student Profile', 'Organization Profile']}>
  <Tab value="Student Profile">
### Student Profile Setup

**Essential Information:** Add profile photo, write compelling bio highlighting your interests and goals, list contact information.

**Education Background:** School or university name, degree or program of study, expected or actual graduation date, GPA if applicable, relevant coursework.

**Work Experience:** Job titles and companies, project experience and internships, volunteer work, duration and responsibilities, key achievements and outcomes.

**Skills & Portfolio:** Technical skills with proficiency levels, soft skills and competencies, links to GitHub, LinkedIn, personal website, portfolio samples and project showcases.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab value="Organization Profile">
### Organization Profile Setup

**Basic Information:** Official organization name, industry classification and sector, organization size and structure, location and contact details.

**Organization Details:** Mission statement and values, organization history and background, current focus areas and initiatives, website and social media links.

**Project Information:** Types of projects you typically post, skills you frequently need, preferred collaboration models, compensation and stipend policies.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout title="Profile Completion Tip" type="success">
Profiles that are at least 70% complete receive significantly more engagement. Take time to fill out all relevant sections thoroughly.
</Callout>

### Viewing Project Details

Click on any project card to view comprehensive information:

- Detailed project description and objectives
- Required skills and preferred qualifications
- Timeline, duration, and time commitment expectations
- Organization information and background
- Application requirements and process
- Stipend information (if applicable)

## Applying to Projects

Submit applications that showcase your qualifications and enthusiasm.

**Application Process**

1. Review project details thoroughly to ensure alignment with your skills
2. Click the Apply button on the project page
3. Complete the application form with relevant information
4. Explain your interest and approach to the project
5. Highlight relevant experience and skills
6. Submit your application for review
7. Track application status in your dashboard

<Callout title="Application Best Practice" type="warn">
Always customize each application to the specific project. Generic applications are significantly less likely to be accepted than personalized submissions.
</Callout>

## Tips for Success

<Tabs items={['For Students', 'For Organizations']}>
  <Tab value="For Students">
### Student Success Tips

Complete your profile thoroughly before applying to projects. Profiles with comprehensive information receive more opportunities and higher response rates.

Customize each application to demonstrate understanding of specific project requirements and show genuine interest.

Communicate professionally and promptly with organizations. Quick, thoughtful responses demonstrate reliability and commitment.

Meet all deadlines and honor commitments. Building a strong reputation leads to more opportunities and better recommendations.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab value="For Organizations">
### Organization Success Tips

Provide clear, detailed project descriptions with realistic scope and well-defined deliverables. Clear expectations lead to better applications.

Respond promptly to applications and student questions. Timely communication shows professionalism and respect for students' time.

Offer constructive feedback throughout projects. Guidance helps students succeed and develop professionally.

Recognize excellent work with endorsements and verification. This builds student portfolios and encourages quality work.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
