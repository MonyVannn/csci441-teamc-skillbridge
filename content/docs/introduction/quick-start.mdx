# User Guide

This guide will help you navigate and use SkillBridge effectively.

## Creating an Account

1. Visit the SkillBridge homepage
2. Click on **Sign Up**
3. Fill in your information
4. Verify your email address

## Setting Up Your Profile

After creating an account, it's important to complete your profile:

### Profile Information
- Add your profile picture
- Write a compelling bio
- List your skills and interests

### Education
Add your educational background:
- School/University name
- Degree or program
- Graduation date
- GPA (optional)

### Experience
Showcase your work experience:
- Job title
- Company/Organization
- Duration
- Description of responsibilities

## Browsing Projects

### Search and Filter
Use the search bar and filters to find projects that match your interests:
- **Search by keyword**: Enter project titles, descriptions, or skills
- **Filter by category**: Select project categories
- **Filter by status**: Active, completed, or upcoming projects

### Project Details
Click on any project to view:
- Project description
- Required skills
- Timeline and commitment
- Organization information

## Applying to Projects

1. Navigate to the project you're interested in
2. Click the **Apply** button
3. Fill out the application form
4. Submit your application
5. Track your application status in your profile

## Using the Chat Feature

Communicate with organizations in real-time:
- Click the chat icon at the bottom right
- Start a conversation with an organization
- Receive notifications for new messages
- View conversation history
