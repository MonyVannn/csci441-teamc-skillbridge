## Email Verification

### Why Verify?
Email verification ensures:
- Account security
- Ability to receive important notifications
- Prevents spam accounts
- Confirms you have access to the email

### Verification Steps

1. **Check Your Inbox**
   - Look for email from SkillBridge (noreply@skillbridge.com)
   - Subject: "Verify Your SkillBridge Account"
   - Email typically arrives within 2-5 minutes

2. **Open the Verification Email**
   - Click the **Verify Email** button
   - Or copy/paste the verification link into your browser

3. **Confirm Verification**
   - You'll be redirected to SkillBridge
   - See a "Email Verified Successfully" message
   - You can now access all features

### Didn't Receive the Email?

If the verification email doesn't arrive:

**Check These First:**
1. âœ… **Spam/Junk Folder** - Sometimes emails get filtered
2. âœ… **Promotions Tab** - In Gmail, check the Promotions category
3. âœ… **Email Address** - Verify you entered it correctly
4. âœ… **Wait Time** - Allow up to 10 minutes for delivery

**Request New Verification:**
1. Log in to your account
2. You'll see a banner: "Email not verified"
3. Click **Resend Verification Email**
4. Check your inbox again

**Still Having Issues?**
- Check your email provider's blocked senders list
- Add noreply@skillbridge.com to your contacts
- Contact our support team

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### "Email Already Registered"
**Problem**: Email address is already in use  
**Solutions**:
- Try signing in instead of signing up
- Use "Forgot Password" to recover your account
- Contact support if you believe this is an error
- Check if you registered with a social login

### "Verification Link Expired"
**Problem**: Clicked verification link after 24 hours  
**Solution**:
- Request a new verification email
- Links expire for security after 24 hours
- New link will be sent immediately

### "Invalid Email Format"
**Problem**: Email not accepted during registration  
**Solutions**:
- Check for typos in email address
- Remove spaces before/after email
- Ensure email follows format: name@domain.com
- Some special characters may not be supported

### "Weak Password Error"
**Problem**: Password doesn't meet requirements  
**Solution**:
- Review password requirements above
- Add uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
- Increase length to 12+ characters for better security
