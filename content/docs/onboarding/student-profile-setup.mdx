## Bio / About Me

**Length**: 2-4 sentences (150-300 characters)

**Structure:**
1. **Who you are**: Your current status (student, graduate, etc.)

3. **What you're looking for**: Type of projects/experience
4. **Your unique value**: What sets you apart
