# Building Your Portfolio

Your SkillBridge projects are valuable portfolio pieces. Learn to showcase them effectively.

## Documenting Projects

### During the Project

<Callout type="warn" title="Document as You Go - Don't Wait!">
  **Capture Everything While It's Fresh:**
  - **Initial requirements** and project brief
  - **Your approach and process** at each stage
  - **Challenges faced** and how you overcame them
  - **Solutions implemented** with reasoning
  - **Final deliverables** and all versions
  - **Feedback received** from organization
  
  It's much easier than trying to recreate everything after completion!
</Callout>

<Tabs items={['Visual Documentation', 'Save Communications']}>
  <Tab value="Visual Documentation">
    **Take Screenshots Throughout:**
    - **Work in progress** at different stages
    - **Final product** from multiple angles
    - **Analytics/results** (if applicable)
    - **Before and after comparisons** showing impact
    - **Team collaboration** tools and workflows
    
    Visuals tell the story better than words alone.
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Save Communications">
    **Archive Positive Feedback:**
    - Positive feedback and appreciation messages
    - Milestone approvals and sign-offs
    - Appreciation messages from team
    - Recommendation letters or endorsements
    - Client testimonials
    
    These validate your work and professionalism.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Quantifying Impact

### Metrics That Matter by Field

<Tabs items={['Web Development', 'Design', 'Marketing', 'Writing']}>
  <Tab value="Web Development">
    **Web Development Metrics:**
    - **Page load time improvement** (e.g., "Reduced from 3.2s to 1.1s")
    - **Bounce rate reduction** (e.g., "Decreased by 35%")
    - **Conversion rate increase** (e.g., "Improved sign-ups by 120%")
    - **Accessibility score** (e.g., "Achieved WCAG 2.1 AA compliance")
    - **Performance metrics** (Lighthouse scores, Core Web Vitals)
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Design">
    **Design Metrics:**
    - **User testing results** (e.g., "95% task completion rate")
    - **Engagement increase** (e.g., "40% more time on page")
    - **Brand recognition lift** (survey results)
    - **A/B testing results** (which design performed better)
    - **Client satisfaction score** (e.g., "5/5 stars")
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Marketing">
    **Marketing Metrics:**
    - **Engagement rate improvement** (likes, comments, shares)
    - **Follower growth** (e.g., "200% increase in 3 months")
    - **Lead generation numbers** (e.g., "Generated 150 qualified leads")
    - **Campaign ROI** (return on investment)
    - **Reach and impressions** (how many people saw it)
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Writing">
    **Writing Metrics:**
    - **Page views** (e.g., "Article reached 10,000 views")
    - **Engagement time** (average time on page)
    - **Social shares** (how widely it was shared)
    - **SEO rankings** (keyword positions improved)
    - **Conversion rates** (if applicable)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Portfolio Maintenance

### Keep It Current

<Callout type="info" title="Regular Portfolio Updates">
  **Every 3-6 Months, Review:**
  - **Add new projects** as you complete them
  - **Remove outdated work** that no longer represents your skills
  - **Update skills and tools** as you learn new technologies
  - **Refresh design** periodically to stay modern
  - **Fix broken links** and images
</Callout>

**Quality Over Quantity:**
- **Showcase your best work** (5-10 projects is ideal)
- **Remove weaker early projects** as you grow
- **Focus on recent, relevant work** for your target role
- **Demonstrate growth and range** across different types of projects

### Portfolio Review Checklist

<Callout type="warn" title="Portfolio Health Check">
  **Every 3-6 Months, Verify:**
  - All links working and going to correct destinations
  - Images loading properly and optimized
  - Contact information current
  - Resume updated with latest experience
  - New projects added with case studies
  - Weak or outdated projects removed
  - Skills list current and accurate
  - About section reflects your current goals
  - No typos or grammatical errors
  - Mobile-friendly and responsive
  - Fast loading on all devices
</Callout>
