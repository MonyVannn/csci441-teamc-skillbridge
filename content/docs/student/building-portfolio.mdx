# Building Your Portfolio

Your SkillBridge projects are valuable portfolio pieces. Learn to showcase them effectively.

## Why Portfolio Matters

### Professional Impact

**Portfolios Demonstrate:**
- Real-world experience
- Practical skills application
- Problem-solving abilities
- Professional work quality
- Diverse project experience

**Benefits:**
- Stand out in job applications
- Prove capabilities to employers
- Show growth over time
- Build professional credibility
- Create talking points for interviews

## Documenting Projects

### During the Project

**Capture Everything:**
- Initial requirements
- Your approach and process
- Challenges faced
- Solutions implemented
- Final deliverables
- Feedback received

**Take Screenshots:**
- Work in progress
- Final product
- Analytics/results (if applicable)
- Before and after comparisons
- Team collaboration

**Save Communications:**
- Positive feedback
- Milestone approvals
- Appreciation messages

**When to Create:**
- Significant projects
- Complex problems solved
- Impressive results
- Unique challenges

**Case Study Structure:**

**1. Overview (200 words)**
- Project summary
- Your role
- Timeline
- Technologies

**2. Background (300 words)**
- Client/organization context
- Problem statement
- Goals and objectives

## Portfolio Maintenance

### Keep It Current

**Regular Updates:**
- Add new projects as completed
- Remove outdated work
- Update skills and tools
- Refresh design periodically
- Fix broken links

**Quality Over Quantity:**
- Showcase your best work (5-10 projects)
- Remove weaker early projects
- Focus on recent, relevant work
- Demonstrate growth and range

### Portfolio Review Checklist

**Every 3-6 Months:**
- [ ] All links working
- [ ] Images loading properly
- [ ] Contact information current
- [ ] Resume updated
- [ ] New projects added
- [ ] Weak projects removed
- [ ] Skills list current
- [ ] About section accurate
- [ ] No typos or errors
- [ ] Mobile-friendly
- [ ] Fast loading
